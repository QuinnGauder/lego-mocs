             A bust of Jinx from the acclaimed Netflix series: Arcane. The inspiration for this build was Jinx's cool hair design; I wanted to make it as accurate as was possible with lego bricks, regardless of the size of the final result. Due to the topheavy design required of a bust of a stylized character like this, I hid multiple layers of structural technic connections in the hair braids and neck to counterbalance it. I am especially proud of the shaping of the hair, body, and ears specifically; keep an eye out for nice part usages like a pirates hat and turban.
