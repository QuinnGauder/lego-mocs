Astro Bot Poseable Puppet

Dimensions (L⋅W⋅H): 24.2cm x 17.9cm x 27.1cm

            A poseable puppet figurine of the loveable protagonist from Astro Bot and it's prequels. Astro can wave/sit on his own, and can dance/stand when being held. The eye tiles can be swapped out to recreate Astro's iconic expressive features. This model was designed for LEGO Ideas with the awesome Astro Bot community in mind.
