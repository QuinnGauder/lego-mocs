CIS Supertank

Pieces: ~765
Dimensions (L⋅W⋅H): 20.3cm x 11.1cm x ~12.1cm
Additional Links: Rebrickable Instructions

             A roughly minifigure scale recreation of the prototype heavily armoured CIS Supertank from Star Wars. The armoured shield can drop to offer maximum protection, or raise to reveal additional heavy firepower. The twin laser cannons on the side of the tank can rotate indepentantly to keep up long distance attacks while closing the gap fully protected This model was originally created for Solid Brix Studio's Supertank contest, for which the colour scheme and footprint were predefined.
