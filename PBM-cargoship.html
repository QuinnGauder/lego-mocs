Cargo Spaceship

Pieces: 254
Dimensions (L⋅W⋅H): 33.2cm x 6.2cm x 6.2cm
Additional Links: Rebrickable Instructions, Lego Ideas Challenge (Ended)

             Inspired by the ocean cargo ships of the modern day, this build re-imagines what these ships would look like if they were equipped for interplanetary space travel. Adorning the back of the ship is a giant rocket engine designed to provide constant acceleration. A white claw arm moves the colourful magnetized shipping containers from the ship's floor to atmospheric ground transport at end of the journey. This build was designed for the "Exploring The Cosmos" Lego Ideas challenge; the goal was to create a space exploration build with 150-250 pieces.
