Robo-Saurus Collection 1

Pieces: 26 (Pterosaur), 58 (Parasaurolophus), 67 (Apatosaurus), 106 (Stegosaurus)
Instructions: Pterosaur, Parasaurolophus, Apatosaurus, Stegosaurus

             Small figurine recreations of a collection of famous dinosaurs / ancient creatures reimagined in a robotic style. Originally inspired by a parts-lite Twitter MOC building challenge, creating well defined silhouttes of the creatures with a minimalism in piece counts and using common part colours were the main goals of these builds. Taking photos of the builds in their respective biomes has also been a pleasure with these micro builds (the final photos in this collection).
