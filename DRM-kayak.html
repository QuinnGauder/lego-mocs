A Kayaking Adventure

Pieces: 1228
Dimensions (L⋅W⋅H): 42.6cm x 28.8cm x 10.8cm
Additional Links: Lego Ideas Submission (Ended)

             A diorama showing a fun adventurous kayaking trip down white-water rapids! Beware the steep drops and mossy rock outcroppings! This build was originally designed for the "An escape, a reset, in the A-Frame Cabin" Lego Ideas challenge, wherein the challenge was to recreate something fun you could do while staying at an A-Frame cabin. My favourite part of this build is the dynamic angle the front kayaker is placed in, with the angled bar connection seamlessly hidden amongst water splashes.
